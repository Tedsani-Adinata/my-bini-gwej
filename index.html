Tendou Kei

An AI who initially resided within Tendou Alice, "Princess" (王女) of the Nameless Gods, now as a Paranormal Affairs Department member. Her purpose was to "lead the Princess to her throne" and bring Kivotos to its end, but her priority is to keep the Princess safe, even if it meant sacrificing herself.

Goryou Nagusa

Goryou Nagusa (御稜ナグサ) is one of the students studying in Hyakkiyako Alliance Academy who wields a Sniper Rifle.

The responsible and highly respected vice president of the Hyakkaryouran Conflict Resolution Council. However, she claims to not be vice president and is only wearing Hyakkaryouran attire as cosplay.

Kirifuji Nagisa

Kirifuji Nagisa (桐藤ナギサ) is one of the students studying in Trinity General School who wields a Handgun.

One of the three presidents of the Tea Party, Trinity's student council, and the leader of the Filius Faction. Nagisa is also Mika's childhood friend. In contrast to Mika, Nagisa thinks first before acting, sometimes thinking a bit too much to the point where she causes problems for others who are uninvolved or are supposed to be considered innocent. Compared to the other Tea Party members, Nagisa's political prowess seems to be the highest.

Sorasaki Hina

Sorasaki Hina (空崎ヒナ) is one of the students studying at Gehenna Academy who wields a Machine Gun.

She is a 3rd year student and the current chairwoman of Gehenna's Prefect Team, who is rightfully feared by every student in the academy, if not all of Kivotos.

At heart, she is a troublesome, laid-back person, who calls it a "hassle" whenever she talks about school rules. However, said person becomes strict at the academy, and enacts calm and quick judgment without hesitation on the battlefield.

Her fighting prowess is astounding and for that reason, any organization that opposes Gehenna is more afraid of her than anyone else from the Prefect Team.

Morizuki Suzumi

Morizuki Suzumi (守月スズミ) is one of the students studying in Trinity General School who wields an Assault Rifle.

Due to the frequent incidents of Trinity students being attacked by other schools, the Vigilante Corps began patrolling around town in defense of this. Her burning sense of justice often leads her to be misunderstood as cold-hearted and cruel student, but she is actually a girlish girl at her age who loves cute things.

Asuma Toki

Asuma Toki (飛鳥馬トキ) is one of the students studying at Millennium Science School who wields an Assault Rifle.

Toki is the mysterious fifth member of C&C with the codename "04" (Zero-Four) in Millennium. Unlike the rest of C&C members, she is assigned as Rio's personal bodyguard at Seminar. As such, she is equipped with special armaments designed by Rio.

The first is the "Mode 1" uniform, which takes a form as a regular maid uniform. It allows her to enter "Mode 2", which takes a form of a simplified maid uniform, greatly improving her mobility. The second is a large powered exoskeleton named "Abi-Eshuh" (アビ・エシェフ) that contains heavy weaponry and the ability to utilize Eridu's entire electricity grid and processing power.
